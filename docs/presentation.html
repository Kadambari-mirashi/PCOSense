01 — Problem

Why PCOSense?

PCOS: A Silent Epidemic

  • Affects 1 in 10 women of reproductive age
  • Up to 70% of cases go undiagnosed
  • Requires multiple specialist visits & expensive labs
  • Early detection prevents metabolic complications

Gaps in Current Care

  • No accessible screening tools in primary care
  • Clinicians lack time to synthesize multi-factor evidence
  • Patients wait months for specialist referrals
  • Existing risk calculators offer no explainability

02 — Solution

PCOSense Overview

PCOSense is a clinical decision-support tool that estimates PCOS risk from 13 patient biomarkers using explainable ML and a multi-agent AI system — delivering an evidence-backed screening in under 30 seconds.
0.95 Model AUROC
3 Specialized AI Agents
27 Clinical Papers (RAG)

Stakeholders: Primary care physicians • Gynecologists • Patients seeking preliminary screening

06 — Quality Control (cont.)

Evidence of AI Performance

Quantitative Evidence

  • AUROC 0.9528 on held-out test set
  • Per-assessment QC scores in UI
  • /api/v1/quality-summary aggregate endpoint
  • Validation flags catch bad inputs pre-prediction
  • Full audit trail in Supabase

Reliability Safeguards

  • SHAP explainability for every prediction
  • LLM fallback: OpenAI → Ollama
  • Dual validation (programmatic + LLM)
  • RAG grounding prevents hallucination
  • NHANES population comparison
  • PubMed integration for current evidence
Users see QC scores, validation flags, and confidence levels alongside results — full transparency.
